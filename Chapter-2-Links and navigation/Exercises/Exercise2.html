Plot

"Friends" is a popular American sitcom that follows the lives of six friends living in New York City as they navigate through the ups and downs of adulthood, relationships, and careers. The show explores themes of friendship, love, and the challenges of growing up. The series spans across ten seasons, portraying the evolving dynamics and hilarious misadventures of the central group.
